    About Me

    Hi, my name is Emile Collins. I am a naturally curious person, always questioning the things we take for granted, such as why the sky is blue or if breakfast is the 'most important meal of the day'? Irrespective of the subject, whether it's religion, politics, drugs, sex or science. I'm interested in all of them and they form an important part of our society, even though nothing is absolutely good or bad, I intend to explore the grey areas. So join me on my journey through some of the most interesting and controversial subjects, start the conversation and lets start thinking about this life!
